PsychologyGeo

Brief

This project is about creating a map that only shows what is necessary to communicate certain information on the subject, and also can use experimental tools with all kinds of techniques that support this.

Visualization Process

I personally would like to describe this project as a practice to visualize information.

I choose to use the top view of the AR molding of my apartment to map my and my roommate's cats' territories in our apartment. The brown line shows my cat DoDo's movement, and the blue line is about my roommate's cat ToTo's movement.
The cutout images of them show what they usually do in every specific place.
